## General Questions

### What is Timebreez?

Timebreez is a staff scheduling and leave management system built for childcare centres. It combines:

- **Roster building**: Create weekly schedules with a visual drag-and-drop interface
- **Leave management**: Staff request time off, managers approve, balances track automatically
- **WhatsApp notifications**: Staff receive schedules and reminders on their phones
- **Payroll export**: Generate payroll-ready data in minutes, not hours

### Who is Timebreez for?

Timebreez is designed for:

- **Childcare centres** (nurseries, creches, preschools)
- **Centre managers** who build rosters and approve leave
- **Childcare staff** who need to know their schedules and request time off

### How is Timebreez different from other scheduling tools?

Timebreez is built specifically for childcare:

- **Room-based scheduling** rather than just generic shifts
- **WhatsApp as the primary staff interface** - no app to download
- **Simple enough** for non-technical staff to use
- **Affordable** - half the cost of tools like Timetastic

### What devices can I use Timebreez on?

**For managers (admin):**
- Web browser on desktop or laptop (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Tablet browsers work too

**For staff:**
- WhatsApp on any smartphone
- Optionally: web browser at [app.timebreez.com](https://app.timebreez.com)

### Is my data secure?

Yes. Timebreez uses:

- 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit
- Encrypted data storage
- Secure authentication
- Regular security updates

Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

## Account Questions

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to [app.timebreez.com](https://app.timebreez.com)
2. Click "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

{: .note }
Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If yours has expired, request a new one.

### How do I change my email address?

1. Log in at [app.timebreez.com](https://app.timebreez.com)
2. Go to **Settings > Profile**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Click **Save**
5. Verify your new email by clicking the link sent to it

### How do I update my phone number for WhatsApp?

Ask your manager to update your phone number in the system. This ensures you receive WhatsApp notifications at the right number.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact your centre manager. For data protection and record-keeping, only administrators can remove accounts.

## Schedule Questions

### How far in advance can I see my schedule?

This depends on when your manager publishes the roster. Typically:

- Schedules are published 1-2 weeks in advance
- You'll receive your weekly schedule every Sunday evening
- You can ask your manager about their scheduling timeline

### Why can't I see next week's schedule?

Your manager may not have published it yet. Check back later or ask your manager when they typically publish schedules.

### Can I sync my schedule with Google Calendar or Apple Calendar?

Yes! To set up calendar sync:

1. Log in at [app.timebreez.com](https://app.timebreez.com)
2. Go to **Settings > Calendar Sync**
3. Copy your personal calendar URL
4. In your calendar app, add a new subscription calendar
5. Paste the URL

Your shifts will appear automatically and update when your schedule changes.

### What if my schedule is wrong?

Contact your manager directly. They can make corrections in the system, and you'll receive an updated notification.

### What if I can't work a scheduled shift?

Contact your manager as soon as possible. Options include:

- Finding someone to cover for you
- Swapping shifts with a colleague (manager approves)
- Taking leave if appropriate

## Leave and Time-Off Questions

### How do I request leave?

**Via WhatsApp (easiest):**
Send a message like "I need December 15-16 off"

**Via the web:**
1. Log in at [app.timebreez.com](https://app.timebreez.com)
2. Click **Request Leave**
3. Select dates and submit

[Full guide: How to Request Leave](/docs/staff/leave/)

### How do I check my leave balance?

**Via WhatsApp:**
Send "balance" or "what's my balance"

**Via the web:**
Log in and go to **My Leave**

### How long does leave approval take?

Most requests are processed within 1-2 business days. For urgent requests, contact your manager directly.

### Can I cancel a leave request?

**If it's still pending:**
Cancel through the web portal or ask your manager.

**If it's already approved:**
Contact your manager to discuss cancelling.

### Why was my leave request denied?

Common reasons include:

- Too many staff already off that day
- Critical staffing period
- Insufficient leave balance
- Not enough notice given

Your denial notification will include the reason. Speak with your manager if you have questions.

### What happens if I'm sick?

1. Reply "SICK" to any no-show alert you receive
2. Or contact your manager directly
3. The sick day is logged in the system
4. No need to formally "request" sick leave

### Do I need a doctor's note for sick leave?

This depends on your centre's policy. Check with your manager about when documentation is required.

## WhatsApp Questions

### Why am I not receiving WhatsApp messages?

Check these common causes:

1. **Phone number**: Is the correct number in the system? Ask your manager.
2. **WhatsApp installed**: Make sure WhatsApp is installed and working
3. **Not blocked**: Ensure the Timebreez number isn't blocked
4. **Notifications enabled**: Check your phone's notification settings

### Can I opt out of WhatsApp notifications?

Talk to your manager. Some notifications may be required, but preferences like morning reminders can often be adjusted.

### What number do messages come from?

Messages come from Timebreez's WhatsApp Business account. The number will be provided when you're onboarded.

### Can I respond to WhatsApp messages?

Yes! You can:

- Reply "SICK" or "LATE" to no-show alerts
- Send "balance" to check your leave balance
- Send "schedule" to see your upcoming shifts
- Send leave requests (e.g., "I need Friday off")

### What if I replied incorrectly?

Contact your manager to make any corrections.

## Troubleshooting
{: #troubleshooting }

### I'm not receiving notifications

**Check these steps:**

1. **WhatsApp notifications**
   - Ensure WhatsApp is installed and notifications are enabled
   - Check the Timebreez number isn't blocked
   - Ask your manager to verify your phone number

2. **Email notifications**
   - Check your spam/junk folder
   - Add noreply@timebreez.com to your contacts

3. **System settings**
   - Ask your manager to check your notification preferences in the system

### The app is running slowly

Try these steps:

1. **Clear your browser cache** (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Delete)
2. **Try a different browser** (Chrome usually works best)
3. **Check your internet connection**
4. **Close other tabs** that might be using memory

### I can't log in

1. **Check your email address** - are you using the correct one?
2. **Reset your password** - click "Forgot password?" on the login page
3. **Check for typos** in your password
4. **Contact your manager** if you're still stuck

### My schedule shows no shifts

This usually means:

- Your manager hasn't published the roster yet
- You haven't been assigned to any shifts
- You might be looking at the wrong date range

Check with your manager if you believe this is an error.

### I received a no-show alert but I was working

Contact your manager immediately. There may be a clock-in issue or a roster mismatch that needs correcting.

## For Managers

### How do I add a new staff member?

1. Go to **Staff > All Staff**
2. Click **Add Employee**
3. Enter their details (name, email, phone)
4. Set their leave allowances
5. They'll receive an invitation email

[Full guide: Admin Setup](/docs/admin/)

### How do I change someone's leave allowance?

1. Go to **Staff > All Staff**
2. Click on the employee
3. Go to **Leave Allowances** tab
4. Update their allowances
5. Save

### How do I set up WhatsApp for my centre?

[See the WhatsApp Setup Guide](/docs/admin/whatsapp/)

### Can I have multiple administrators?

Yes. Go to **Settings > Users** to add additional admin users with different permission levels.

## Billing Questions

### How does billing work?

Timebreez is billed monthly or annually based on your centre size. Contact your account representative for specific pricing.

### How do I get an invoice?

Invoices are sent automatically to your billing email. You can also access them in **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and direct debit for annual plans.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Contact support@timebreez.com to discuss cancellation. We'd also love to hear any feedback about why you're leaving.
